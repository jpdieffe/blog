Monte Carlo Photon Transport Simulation

Simulates photon propagation through a scattering/absorbing medium. Photons launch from the source (left), scatter via Henyey-Greenstein phase function, and are detected if they re-emerge at the surface within the detector radius. Detected paths are rendered on the plot.
