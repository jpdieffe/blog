Near-Infrared Spectroscopy and Electroencephalography Enabled Headband for Unobtrusive Microarousal Detection and Sleep Quality Estimation

Defense Health Agency (DHA) SBIR Phase I Start Date: January 2024

Phase I SBIR project developing an innovative headband that combines near-infrared spectroscopy (NIRS) and electroencephalography (EEG) for unobtrusive detection of microarousals and estimation of sleep quality. This technology aims to provide advanced sleep monitoring capabilities for defense and clinical applications, enabling better assessment of sleep health and performance impacts.
